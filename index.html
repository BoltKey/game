Rules

There is a number of mechanics you should understand in order to win by defeating all monsters. Don't forget: Exploring the rules is part of the fun! You may need multiple attempts before you manage to win.

Goal of the game

Defeat all monsters. Your final score is amount of your coins plus 3 times the amount of your diamonds.

Turn structure

At the end of every turn, several things happen:
  1. Convert swords to coins: lose all your swords and gain the same amount of coins
  2. Activate summoned cards: do all the effects on all the cards you have summoned so far
  3. Activate monsters: there are monsters coming to your castle. All the monsters move one step closer to the castle. Monster that are already in front of your castle take ğŸ’° according to their strength. If you don't have enough resources, you lose the game.
